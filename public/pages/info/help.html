Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?expand_more

Click Create Account, fill in your name, university email, and password. You'll receive a verification email — click the link to activate your account.

I didn't receive the verification emailexpand_more

Check your spam/junk folder. If it's not there, go to the login page and click "Resend Verification Email". Make sure you entered the correct email address during registration.

How do I edit my profile?expand_more

Go to Profile to edit your name, bio, skills, avatar, banner, social links, and privacy settings. Your profile completion percentage updates in real-time as you fill in more details.

How do I change my password?expand_more

Go to Settings → Account section → Change Password. Enter your current password and your new password. If you forgot your password, use the Forgot Password link on the login page.

How do I delete my account?expand_more

Go to Settings → Danger Zone → Delete Account. This action is permanent and cannot be undone. You can export your data before deletion.

How do I create a team?expand_more

Go to Teams → Create Team. Fill in your team name, description, required skills, and max members. You can then invite people to join your team.

How does messaging work?expand_more

Go to Messages to chat with your friends. Messages are delivered in real-time using WebSockets. You can see online status and "last seen" timestamps for your contacts.

Can I message anyone?expand_more

You can message anyone who has accepted your friend request. Send a friend request first from the People page, and once accepted, you'll be able to start a conversation.
