Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2026

At ProjectHive, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

database 1. Information We Collect

settings 2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and maintain the ProjectHive platform and its features.
  • To personalize your experience — suggest teammates, relevant content, and trending topics.
  • To communicate with you — account verification, password resets, and important updates.
  • To ensure security — detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access.
  • To improve the platform — analyze usage patterns and optimize features.

share 3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We may share limited data in these cases:

  • With your consent — when you explicitly agree to share information.
  • Service providers — hosting (Render/Vercel), database (Supabase), email services — who process data on our behalf.
  • Legal requirements — if required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

lock 4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt — we never store plain-text passwords.
  • API access is protected with JWT authentication.
  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS encryption.
  • We use rate limiting to prevent brute-force attacks.
  • Database access is restricted with Row Level Security (RLS) policies.

visibility 5. Your Privacy Controls

You have full control over your privacy through the Settings page:

  • Profile visibility — choose to make your profile public or private.
  • Email visibility — control whether your email is shown on your profile.
  • Online status — choose whether friends can see when you're online.
  • Friend requests — enable or disable incoming friend requests.
  • Discoverability — control whether you appear in "Find People" searches.

delete 6. Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time:

  • Go to Settings → Danger Zone → Delete Account to permanently delete your data.
  • You can also export your data as JSON before deletion.
  • After deletion, your data is permanently removed within 30 days.

cookie 7. Cookies & Local Storage

ProjectHive uses localStorage to store:

  • Authentication token — to keep you logged in.
  • Theme preference — light/dark mode setting.
  • Cached data — profile info and feed data for faster loading.
  • Bookmarks — your saved posts list.

We do not use third-party tracking cookies.

child_care 8. Children's Privacy

ProjectHive is intended for users aged 16 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16. If we discover such data, we will delete it immediately.

mail 9. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions or concerns, contact us at privacy@projecthive.com or visit our Help Center.
