About ProjectHive

ProjectHive is a student collaboration platform built to connect university students across Bangladesh — find teammates, share achievements, and build amazing projects together.

stars Our Mission

We believe the best projects are built by diverse teams. ProjectHive was created to solve a common problem — university students often struggle to find the right teammates for hackathons, thesis projects, and startup ideas. Our platform bridges that gap by connecting students based on skills, interests, and goals.
